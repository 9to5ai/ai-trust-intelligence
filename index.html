How to read this terminal

Everything here is produced by a nightly research cycle over public sources, then rendered from a single data file.

Priority & severity

Critical Act now — material exposure or a dated legal obligation.
High Plan a response this quarter.
Medium Monitor; revisit next cycle.
Low Background awareness.

Confidence & evidence

High Confidence: how sure the research is about the claim.
AU Directly relevant to Australian organisations.
E-A…E-D Evidence grade of the underlying source (A strongest).

Trend arrows

▲ increasing Risk is growing or regulation is tightening.
◆ stable No significant change since last cycle.
▼ decreasing Pressure is easing.

The risk matrix

Rows are likelihood, columns are impact, brighter cells hold more risks. Click a cell to filter the list below it to just those risks.

Keyboard shortcuts

⌘K or / — search everything. [ / ] — previous / next section. esc — close dialogs.
